Interpretation checklist:
- smoke/quick: pipeline check only, not scientific evidence.
- course/core: first useful original-baseline vs M3-gate comparison.
- evidence_m3: controlled same-setup M3-original comparison against the M3-gate run.
- evidence_ablation: checks whether gate temperature/sparsity terms contribute to results.
- ablation/full: stronger evidence if trends are consistent across seeds/datasets.
- speedup claims require runtime evidence and GPU_MONITOR=1 logs when discussing GPU memory/utilization.
- gate_loss/gate_sparsity/gate_entropy/gate_diversity are logged for new M3-gate runs.
- active-edge claims still require extra instrumentation beyond aggregate gate statistics.
- If gate entropy stays near 0.693, report that the gate remains high-entropy rather than claiming hard edge selection.
